Welcome to Chalk's Farm

Chalk's Farm Holiday Park is set in a spectacular location close to West Wittering's famous beach. We are privately owned and set in beautiful rural surroundings in West Sussex.

Situated near the South Downs, only 1.5 miles away from the sandy beaches at West Wittering and the water sports facilities and natural environment of Chichester Harbour.

We have a selection of 4 - 8 berth caravans available to rent, each one is spacious and modern with fully-equipped fitted kitchens and private shower facilities.

Facilities on-site include a children's playground and a farm shop selling fresh bread, milk, eggs and butter.
